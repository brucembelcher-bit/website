Have You Thanked a Trucker Today?

During this busy holiday season trucks cover the roads, going this way, going that way, delivering gizmos and gadgets across the world. Have you considered thanking those faithful drivers? It is simple: see a truck, pull over, stop walking, or going where you are going and THANK them. Wave, scream, profoundly nod to them. We no longer live in a world where products are made, food is grown, or a world where our economy is built on the backs of the hard workers doing their hard jobs. But, that does still exist in truckers. Truckers love to be thanked, they thrive on receiving praise and gratitude. And you may discover that it feels good to send thanks too. On behalf of BeBruce.Org please consider thanking a trucker.
